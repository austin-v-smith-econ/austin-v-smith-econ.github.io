Welcome! I am a postdoctoral researcher at Northeastern University, working under the supervision of Drs. Matthew B. Ross and CarlyWill Sloan. I received my PhD in Economics from the University of Arizona. [CV]

I am a labor economist with research interests in the criminal justice system. My work addresses policy-relevant questions about the incentives, objectives, and information environments of the workers within this system, with a particular focus on police officers. I was a Graduate Research Fellow for the National Institute of Justice. To learn more about my research, click here.
